Workshops and Training

On-site training for your team

Offering continuous education opportunities is slowly becoming a large part of my work. I do this by hosting individuals, groups or clients at workshops and training events where I teach and train any number of people how to write, design and build better, more scalable decentralized systems on decentralized protocols.

By providing a customised training and workshops experience - the objective is to provide to a training lesson that actually lasts. Customization ensures that any team gets the most relevant and pertinent information possible, by addressing real world challenges, by delivering it effectively and lastly by reinforcing and regularly using the lessons of the trainings and workshops.

Decentralized Protocol Systems

A decentralized approach to the internet brings forth many opportunities. The goal of my workshops is to bring together enthusiasts and practitioners to analyze and discuss the huge potential, but also the inevitable limitations of decentralized systems and security. During our time together, groups will learn about:

  • applying blockchains and DLT to network infrastructure;
  • the moral and business impact of decentralization;
  • Peer-to-Peer (P2P) security and privacy;
  • how decentralized networks really work, and how to play to its strengths and weaknesses;
  • decentralized trust and rights management, including access control;
  • secure Service provisioning and migration in decentralized environments
  • decentralized authentication and access management;
  • application of concepts from outside of Web3.0 to decentralized security;
  • and much, much more.

Decentralization has become a key enabling design goal of network systems. Recent advances in DLT and Blockchains can be incorporated to support decentralized businesses which in turn are becoming increasingly serious in large-scale deployments, such as smart cities.
